Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Nexus ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Gmail integration service.

Gmail Integration

Our AI agent service allows you to send emails through your Gmail account. When you connect your Gmail account, we access your Gmail account solely for the purpose of sending emails on your behalf as requested.

Data Collection

When you connect your Gmail account, we collect and store:

  • OAuth Tokens: We securely store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens to maintain your Gmail connection
  • Account Information: Basic account information (email address) necessary to send emails
  • Email Content: We only access email content when you explicitly request to send an email through our service

How We Use Your Data

  • To send emails on your behalf when you request it through our AI agent
  • To maintain your Gmail connection and refresh authentication tokens
  • We do not read your existing emails unless you explicitly request it
  • We do not share your email data with third parties
  • We do not use your email data for advertising or marketing purposes

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely in our database
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • We follow industry-standard security practices and protocols
  • Access to your Gmail account is limited to sending emails only

Data Retention

We retain your OAuth tokens only as long as your Gmail account remains connected to our service. You can disconnect your Gmail account at any time through the Settings page, which will immediately revoke our access and delete stored tokens.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your Gmail account at any time
  • Request deletion of your stored OAuth tokens
  • Review what data we have access to through your Google account settings

Third-Party Services

Our service uses Google's Gmail API. Your use of Gmail is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
